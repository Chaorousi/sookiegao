Paws Club

喵喵汪汪军团 · long-term stray animal rescue

As president of Paws, I led long-term stray animal rescue through TNR, fundraising, shelter construction, and adoption support. Beyond rescuing individual animals, I hope to promote a more sustainable and compassionate approach to animal welfare within the community.

02

Symbiotic Healing Exhibition

Human · Animal · Nature connectedness

I curated an exhibition that explored the relationship between humans and nature through psychology, biology, and recycled art installations. By transforming environmental ideas into an immersive experience, I wish to encourage visitors to reflect on coexistence, sustainability, and our shared responsibility for the natural world.

03

Barrier-Free Experience Exhibition

“Even without sight, there is still vision.”

I designed immersive simulations that allowed participants to experience barriers faced by people with disabilities, from visual impairment to limited mobility. Combining psychology, environmental science, and technology, the exhibition advocated for more inclusive public spaces and accessible design.

Reading Buddy Club

Weekly English lessons for visually impaired children

As president of the Reading Buddy Club, I have taught visually impaired children English every week while organizing fundraising events and producing a documentary about their experiences. The project reminded me that meaningful inclusion begins with consistent companionship and genuine understanding.
